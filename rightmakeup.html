Complete Guide to Choosing the Right Makeup

Master the art of selecting makeup that enhances your natural beauty

Understanding Your Undertone

Cool Undertones

Blue veins, burns easily, looks best in silver jewelry

Warm Undertones

Green veins, tans easily, looks best in gold jewelry

Neutral Undertones

Mix of warm and cool, looks good in both silver and gold

Makeup Products by Skin Type

Foundation & Concealer

  • Fair Skin: Neutral or pink undertones
  • Medium Skin: Golden or beige tones
  • Deep Skin: Warm caramel to espresso
<

Blush

  • Fair Skin: Soft pinks and peach
  • Medium Skin: Warm apricot or rose
  • Deep Skin: Rich berry or plum
<

Lipstick

  • Fair Skin: Soft nudes and pinks
  • Medium Skin: Mauves and peaches
  • Deep Skin: Bold reds and berries

Final Tips for Flawless Application

  • Always test foundation on your jawline
  • Use primer suited for your skin type
  • Blend properly to avoid harsh lines
  • Experiment with different shades
