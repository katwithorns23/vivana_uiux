Double Cleansing: The Secret to Clearer, Healthier Skin

Discover the transformative power of this two-step cleansing method

What is Double Cleansing?

Step 1: Oil-Based Cleanser
Step 2: Water-Based Cleanser

Double cleansing is a two-step process that involves using an oil-based cleanser first, followed by a water-based cleanser. This ensures a deep, thorough clean by removing both oil-based and water-based impurities.

Why is Double Cleansing Important?

  • Removes stubborn makeup & sunscreen effectively
  • Prevents clogged pores & acne
  • Balances oil production naturally
  • Enhances skincare absorption
