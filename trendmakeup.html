Trending Makeup Looks You Need to Try

Makeup trends are constantly evolving, bringing new and exciting ways to express your style and creativity. Whether you love a soft, natural aesthetic or bold, dramatic glam, there's a trending look for everyone. Here are four of the hottest makeup trends right now and how to achieve them.

Glazed Donut Skin (Dewy, Luminous Glow)

This look is all about achieving a radiant, ultra-glowy complexion that appears fresh and hydrated—like a glazed donut.

  1. Prep your skin with a hydrating serum and moisturizer to create a smooth base.
  2. Apply a lightweight, dewy foundation or skin tint for a fresh, natural look.
  3. Use a liquid highlighter on high points of the face (cheekbones, bridge of the nose, and brow bones) for a luminous effect.
  4. Keep the eyes minimal with neutral, shimmery eyeshadow and soft mascara.
  5. Finish with a glossy lip balm or lip oil for an effortlessly juicy pout.

Siren Eyes (Seductive, Elongated Eye Look)

Inspired by the sultry, fox-eye effect, siren eyes create a lifted, mysterious gaze using sharp, elongated eyeliner and smoky shadows.

  1. Use a brown or black eyeshadow to softly extend the outer corners of the eyes.
  2. Apply a winged eyeliner that extends outward and slightly upward.
  3. Blend the lower lash line with a dark brown or black shadow for depth.
  4. Use a nude or white pencil liner on the waterline to open up the eyes.
  5. Add volumizing mascara or false lashes to enhance the lifted effect.

Strawberry Girl Makeup (Soft, Rosy & Fresh)

This trend embraces a natural, flushed complexion with pink tones, reminiscent of the soft hues of strawberries.

  1. Use a lightweight foundation or skin tint for a sheer, breathable base.
  2. Apply pink or coral cream blush generously on the cheeks and across the nose for a sun-kissed effect.
  3. Keep the eyes simple with light brown or peach eyeshadow and a touch of mascara.
  4. Add freckles using a brow pencil for a youthful, natural touch.
  5. Finish with a tinted lip balm or glossy pink lipstick for a soft and fresh look.

Graphic Eyeliner (Bold, Artistic & Fun)

This trend takes eyeliner to a new level, incorporating geometric shapes, floating creases, and bright colors for an artistic touch.

  1. Use a colored liquid eyeliner (neon, pastel, or metallic) to create unique shapes or floating lines above the crease.
  2. Experiment with double wings or negative space eyeliner for an edgy look.
  3. Keep the rest of the face minimal with a matte or dewy base and soft nude lips.
  4. Add small gems or glitter accents to enhance the artistic effect.

Makeup trends are all about having fun and expressing yourself. Whether you prefer a natural, glowing finish or a bold, statement look, these trending makeup styles will keep your beauty routine fresh and exciting. Try them out and see which one becomes your new go-to look!
