Morning Routine

Step 1: Cleanser
  • Use gentle cleanser suited to your skin type
  • Remove overnight buildup
  • Choose hydrating or foaming formula based on skin type
Step 2: Toner
  • Balance pH levels
  • Use alcohol-free formulations
  • Prep skin for following products
Step 3: Serum & Moisturizer
  • Apply vitamin C or hyaluronic acid serum
  • Lock in with appropriate moisturizer
  • Finish with SPF 30+ protection

Evening Routine

Step 1: Double Cleanse
  • First cleanse with oil-based cleanser
  • Second cleanse with water-based cleanser
  • Ensure all makeup and SPF is removed
Step 2: Treatment
  • Apply retinol or active ingredients
  • Use targeted treatments for specific concerns
  • Allow products to absorb properly
Step 3: Hydration
  • Apply eye cream gently
  • Use richer night moisturizer
  • Consider facial oils for extra nourishment
